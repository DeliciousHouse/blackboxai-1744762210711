Acoustic PanelsÂ 

Our fabric-wrapped panels are crafted with high-quality sound-blocking materials that effectively dampen sound, reduce echo, and enhance clarity. We also offer custom outer-framed panels with premium wood finishes that blend exceptional sound performance with sophisticated aesthetics.

Bass Traps

Also known as corner traps, these acoustic devices create a balanced audio environment by reducing sound reflections. Bass traps are ideal for recording studios, mixing and mastering rooms, home theaters, and music spaces. By converting sound frequencies into heat through friction, they enhance acoustic clarity.

Acoustic Diffusers

Diffusers scatter sound in multiple directions. They effectively manage sound aberrations, reverberations, and reflections by dispersing them both spatially and over time. Ideal for large spaces, diffusers reduce echoes and coloration while preserving a vibrant acoustic atmosphere.

Fabric Tracking System

This method involves the stretching of fabric and securing it over acoustic panels or acoustically insulated walls or ceilings. The result is a seamless, high-end finished look that enhances sound quality and controls noise within the space. It is commonly used in theaters, auditoriums, conference rooms, offices, production studios, hotels, and restaurants.
